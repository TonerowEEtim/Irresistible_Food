Shrimp Tacos

Prepare the taco shells according to the packet’s instructions.

For the shrimp..

Ingredients:
  • Peeled shrimp
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Ginger/garlic
  • Oil for sauteing
Method….

In a pan, add everything and cook stirring occasionally until the shrimp is well cooked and no liquid is left. Remove from the stove and keep aside.

Method:

Peel the avocado and throw away the peel n seed. In a bowl, add the avocado along with the remaining ingredients and mix well. Keep aside.

For the topping:
You need..
  1. Seasoned boiled corn kernels
  2. Chopped lettuce
  3. Chopped onion
  4. Chopped tomato
  5. Grated cheese
  6. Chopped coriander
To assemble..

Fill in the taco shells with shrimp followed by the chopped vegetables mentioned above followed by guacamole n top with cheese.

Enjoy!
