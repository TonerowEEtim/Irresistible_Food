Crab Meat Dip

Crab dip, sometimes referred to as Maryland crab dip, is a thick, creamy dip that is typically prepared from cream cheese and lump crab meat. Other primary ingredients such as mayonnaise may be used. Various types of crab preparations, species and superfamilies are used, as are a variety of added ingredients.

Ingredients:
  • Cooked crab meat.. chopped
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour cream
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Chopped green onions
Method
  1. In a bowl add everything and mix well.
  2. You can add the ingredients in any amount to suit your taste.
  3. Serve as a dip to crackers or as a spread for a sandwich.
  4. Enjoy!
