Minced Beef Tortilla Bowls:
Recipe
Ingredients for the Minced meat..
  • Minced meat
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Chilli flakes
  • Ginger/garlic paste
Method:

Peel the avocado and throw away the peel n seed. In a bowl, add the avocado along with the remaining ingredients and mix well. Keep aside.



For the topping:
  • You need..
  • Seasoned boiled corn kernels
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Chopped onion
To assemble..

Fill in the tortilla bowls with the Cooked minced meat followed by the chopped vegetables mentioned above followed by guacamole n top with cheese. Or you can fill in any order. Serve with a side of lemon wedges.

Enjoy!
