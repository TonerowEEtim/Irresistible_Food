Air Fryer Kebabs

Air Fryer Grilled Chicken Kebabs or kabobs is a quick recipe that uses chicken breasts and fresh veggies to create a healthy meal served on BBQ skewers.

Ingredients:
  • Minced meat
  • Garlic/ginger paste
  • Salt
  • Ground cumin
  • Curry powder
  • Chili flakes
Method:
  1. Mix everything well and shape into oval kebab shapes.
  2. Place the kebabs in the air fryer and cook until well done
  3. Enjoy!
