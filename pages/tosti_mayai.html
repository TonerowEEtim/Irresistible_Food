Tosti Mayai (Egg Toast)

Egg on toast is a popular recipe used to enrich bread among urban dwellers. It is prepared by simply dipping bread slices on beaten egg, then frying on lightly oiled pan. Mainly serves as a breakfast item and is eaten by the entire family
Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, whole (134 g)
  • 1/8 tsp. (1 g) salt, iodized
  • 1/8 cup (28 g) cooking oil
  • 4 slices bread, white (123 g)

Preparation 10 minutes | Cooking 10 minutes | Makes 4

  • Break eggs into a clean bowl, add salt and beat.
  • Heat a wide pan for 1 minute on high heat.
  • Put oil and spread it into the pan. Heat the oil for 1 minute.
  • Dip bread slices into the eggs, turn to coat both sides of each slice.
  • Gently place all the egg-soaked bread slices onto the greased pan.
  • Cook each side for 1 minute.
  • Remove from heat and serve.
